About Me

I have been working in the Information Technology space for the last 16 years. I graduated from the University of Cambridge and CTI with an Information Technology Engineering diploma, with Honours. This course typically takes 3 years to complete, however I was able to achieve this in under a year. I found myself in the fortunate position of being offered a Lecturer position in Information Technology Engineering at CTI after completing my qualification, which afforded me the opportunity to define what my career and future interactions with people would look like.

Two and a half years into instructing, a new opportunity presented itself within the Database Administration space. Since my first humbling experience with MSSQL 7.0, I have had the privilege of working on a number of Database engines within large production environments. These include Microsoft SQL Server, SingleStore (MEMSQL), MySQL, Oracle, SAP MaxDB, AWS RDS, to name a few. Being a specialist in these database engines has given me the proper planning, project management and overall corporate communications skills, but I was also able to build the confidence and experience to manage larger teams. At the beginning of 2016, I had the opportunity to manage a high performing team which consisted from multiple domains, of which Databases, Networks, Microsoft and Linux operations were included..

In 2017, I was offered a position as a Database Engineering Team Lead at Kurtosys Systems, in Cape Town. Kurtosys is a leader in investment management communications with a significant international presence, as well as exciting data and database solutions to work on. Kurtosys has kept me learning and growing for the last 5 years, while leading a high performing team who are driven to provide an excellent user experience and who constantly find value and insights in the data.
